About the Author

Anne Lene Munoru, 58, was born in Kenya and now lives in Germany. A graduate of the Technical University of Munich with an MSc in Agribusiness, Anne is walking through life with courage, despite living with cancer, disability, and past mental health struggles. Her strength comes from faith—and a God-given vision to write beautifully illustrated Bible bedtime stories that inspire children with hope, truth, and courage. Through every page, Anne’s message is clear: no matter what we face, hope is a good thing.
